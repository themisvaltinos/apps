Overview

This policy describes how the Diloti iOS app ("the App") handles your information.

Data and storage

The App does not require account creation and does not collect personal data for core gameplay.

Your settings and game progress are stored locally on your device.

Analytics and tracking

The App does not use advertising SDKs and does not track you across other apps or websites.

Internet usage

Diloti gameplay works fully offline. Network access may only be used for standard App Store related functionality required by Apple.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will change when we do.
