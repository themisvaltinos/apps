Overview

This policy describes how the Habits iOS app ("the App") handles your information. The App is published by the developer identified on the App Store listing.

Data storage

Habits stores your habit data locally on your device. We do not require account creation to use the app.

Your data is used only to provide app features such as check-ins, streaks, planning, and heatmap visualization.

Widgets

If you use Home Screen widgets, the app shares only required habit data through Apple's App Group storage so widgets can display your selected habit.

Analytics and tracking

We do not use third-party advertising SDKs and do not track you across apps or websites.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will change when we do.
