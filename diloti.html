🃏 How to play

In Diloti, you capture cards by matching rank or by combining values. Build and raise declarations, and chase a Xeri (+10) by clearing the entire table in a single capture.

First player to 61 points wins.

📊 Scoring

End-of-round points: most cards (+4), each Ace (+1), 10♦ (+2), 2♣ (+1), each Xeri (+10).

Play fully offline, with no ads and no account required.
