🎮 Game Center & multiplayer

Online play uses Apple Game Center. Sign in under Settings → Game Center on your iPhone.

Multiplayer is available as a one-time in-app purchase. Restore works on any device with the same Apple ID.

🃏 How to play

Match the top card's rank to capture the pile. Jacks capture everything. Score points for Aces, Kings, Queens, Jacks, 10s, and special cards. A Xeri happens when you capture a single card from the table — worth bonus points.
